Sizing

Get the size right the first time.

Our rings come from the finite 2017 production, and we do not offer resizing for them — which makes sizing before you order the most important step. Exchanges are available within 30 days for another in-stock size of the same design. Because inventory is finite, a replacement size may not always be available. Rings returned for a sizing exchange must be unworn and undamaged.

Step One

Request a free ring sizer.

Email remembrancerings1@gmail.com with your mailing address and we’ll send you a free ring sizer, no purchase required. It’s the same method jewelers use, and it takes two minutes once it arrives.

Step Two

Check it against available sizes.

Each ring page lists the exact sizes that physically exist — many are the last of their size. If your size is shown, you’re ready to order. If it isn’t, email us; we’ll tell you honestly whether another design fits you.

Our Policy

No resizing. Honest exchanges.

We do not offer resizing for these rings. Exchanges are available within 30 days for another in-stock size of the same design. Because inventory is finite, a replacement size may not always be available. Rings returned for a sizing exchange must be unworn and undamaged. Please size carefully before ordering, especially for sizes marked “Last one.”
