Ring Story

Remember who you are.

The Child of God ring was created as a quiet reminder of divine identity. On ordinary days, heavy days, and the days when you need to feel grounded again, this ring points back to a simple truth: you are known, loved, and never forgotten.

Material & Availability

Verified material. Exact sizes.

Material

Crafted in Guadalajara from a cobalt–chromium–molybdenum jeweler’s alloy, a metal family known for wear and corrosion resistance. Independent XRF readings from two rings in the 2017 batch identified cobalt, chromium, and molybdenum as the primary metals. Not sterling silver or a precious-metal product. Contains nickel; customers with known metal sensitivities should take note.

Includes a protective black velvet pouch. Unused original inventory from our 2017 Guadalajara production, individually stored and inspected before sale.

Available sizes

Sizes cannot be restocked. We do not offer resizing for these rings. Exchanges are available within 30 days for another in-stock size of the same design. Because inventory is finite, a replacement size may not always be available. Rings returned for a sizing exchange must be unworn and undamaged. Not sure of your size?
